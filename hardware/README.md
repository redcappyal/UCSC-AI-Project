# Fin mount — printable parts (fully-printed revision)

Generated by `generate_mount_stls.py` from the numbers in `../docs/mount-spec.md`
(the spec is the source of truth — edit parameters there/in the script and re-run):

```
python3 hardware/generate_mount_stls.py
```

**Zero purchased hardware.** All joints are printed dovetails + tapered pins; the
clamp is a printed M12×2.5 coarse-thread thumbscrew; bands are printed TPU rings.
Iteration-1 tradeoff: durability for convenience — worn printed parts get reprinted.

0.2 mm layers, 4 perimeters, ≥40% gyroid on saddle_mast. No PLA (creep).
68A pad note: pose stiffness is unaffected by the soft durometer (flat-pad
stiffness scales with area, and these areas are huge); pockets are deeper
(2.5) and sections thicker so soft pads can't squirm in shear.

## Draft status — verify before printing final

- **Placeholders in this revision**: fin-to-wall gap `G = 5` (measure on site);
  **patch-fitting notch is NOT modeled** (F1–F3 unmeasured, spec §5); phone+case
  envelope is a generic 152 × 77 × 10 — replace with the real phone before
  printing the cradle, and re-check the lens-corner opening against the actual
  camera cluster and mic positions (spec §5: P, E, M).
- **No fillets/chamfers yet** — spec §7.1 requires ≥2 mm fillets near glass.
  Acceptable for the first test print.
- The cradle liner (single drop-in TPU boot, spec §7.4) is not generated —
  it needs the real phone envelope first.
- Printed-thread fit varies by printer: if the clamp screw binds, scale the
  screw 99% in the slicer or bump `THR_CLEAR`; if the dovetails rattle, that's
  fine — the pins and glass datums carry the registration.

## Assembly (tool-free)

1. Glue TPU 68A pads into their recesses (CA): seat strip in cap underside,
   jaw pads in channel faces, wall pads on the two bosses, swivel face into
   the pad cap.
2. Snap the pad cap over the clamp screw's ball tip.
3. Thread the clamp screw into the boss (it should turn by hand; see fit note).
4. Slide wedge onto the mast dovetail to the end-stop; drop a tapered pin in.
5. Slide cradle onto the wedge's face dovetail to the end-stop; second pin.
6. On court: hook over the fin, slide to the wall stops, snug the screw,
   seat the phone, stretch both bands on.

Fit checks done in generation: all meshes watertight; dovetail rail/slot and
screw/nut thread sectioned at assembly position with designed clearances.
